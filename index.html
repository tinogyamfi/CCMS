Welcome to Our Saturday Bible & Moral Training Program

About the School

Founded on 1st July 2024 by Very Reverend Samuel Musah, this school is dedicated to training children aged 6 to 16 years in the ways of God and His Kingdom. Our weekly sessions help raise children with strong spiritual and moral foundations.

We meet every Saturday from 11:00 AM to 3:30 PM at Atwima Mim inside Universal International School.

Academics & Training

  • Bible Surveying
  • Study of God, Jesus, and the Holy Spirit
  • Understanding Satan and the Fall of Man
  • Christian Music & Worship
  • Obedience & Respect
  • Time Management
  • And many more Bible-centered teachings
